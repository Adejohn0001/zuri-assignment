Chief Dr. Olusegun Matthew Obansanjo

A great Nigerian politician and theologist who so much believe in Nigeria and have ruled the country as a military Head of State and as a President in the democratic sense of government.

List of notable things done by former President Obasanjo :

  • He brought about the means of communication by introducing GSM
  • He strived to take forward the initiative of reinstating civilian goverment in 1979 along with reformation programs so as to enhance quality of public service.
  • Established association with the US and in 1979 the second republican constitution was structured based on the US Constitution model.
  • On October 1, he peacefully handed over power to the newly elected President of Nigeria in person of Alhaji Shehu Shagari.
  • In his first term as the President of Nigeira he garnered few western supports including the US and the UK.
  • Obasanjo was globally acclaimed for the critical conciliating measures taken by Nigeria
  • Strived to reduced poverty and corruption, aimed to reform police and military while establishing democracy.
  • Obansanjo alongside the president of Cameroon, Paul Biya resolved the dispute of the Bakassi peninsula border by signing the 'Greentree Agreement' on June 12, 2006
  • During his tenure the country experienced an immense economic growth including high growth rate of GDP, higher oil prices and increase in foreign reserves.
  • Nigeria acquired debt pardons from London and Paris that sums up to around $18 billion.
